FarmManagement

Manage every aspect of your farm — from harvest to payroll.

A cloud-based SaaS platform built for Indian farmers and agri-businesses to run their entire operation from a single dashboard. Track harvests, manage your workforce, monitor inventory, and stay on top of finances — with plans that grow with you.

  • Harvest tracking — log sessions, weights, and link produce to inventory in real time
  • Team & payroll — manage permanent and seasonal staff, attendance and leave
  • Inventory — monitor stock, process goods, and record retail sales
  • Live market prices via data.gov.in government data sources
  • Secure billing via Razorpay, role-based access, and 2FA

GraftAI

Stop shipping features blindly. Let your users show you what to build.

An open-source Laravel package that gives multi-tenant SaaS platforms the ability to build and evolve features driven by real user behaviour. Tenants describe what they need in plain English — GraftAI converts that into a live, scheduled data pipeline. No code. No deployment.

  • Natural language → live data pipeline, powered by Claude AI
  • Scheduled pipeline execution with email and webhook triggers
  • Watches tenant patterns and surfaces promotion candidates automatically
  • One-review approval turns a pattern into a versioned, audited platform feature
